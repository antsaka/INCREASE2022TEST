INCREASE in General

INCREASE (industrial creative season) is an annual international competition held by the Faculty of Industrial Engineering, Telkom University since 2007. INCREASE is a series of industrial engineering scientific competitions to accommodate students' interests and talents related to industrial engineering aspects through industrial simulation competition in a real production field.

As one of the most prestigious industrial engineering scientific competitions, INCREASE also implements a production floor simulation that designs an environmentally friendly work system and can minimize waste production in the real industrial world.
